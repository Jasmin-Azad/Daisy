FTFL training program

The initiative of LICT is part of the government's plan to create 30,000 direct and 120,000 indirect jobs in the IT and ITES companies under LICT Project in which the World Bank is providing US$ 70 million credit for implementing the Leveraging ICT for growth, Employment and Governance project (LICT) of Bangladesh Computer Council (BCC) under the ICT Division of Posts, Telecommunication and IT Ministry. Initially the program will be launched for building 840 educated youths as FTFL and befitting to the skills demand of IT and ITES companies by December this year. Out of 840 initial FTFL 240 will be selected for software development (IT) 200 for BPO (ITES) 200 for IT/ITES related management 200 for ITSS (IT Sales & Support)

News/Events

Initial 180 hours intensive General Training & Track Specific General Training will be arranged by LICT Project through a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with one nationally recognized training institute (like BARD). This training is all about basic soft skills & the Track Specific basic courses. Specialized 140-180 hours Course Training will be held on different specialized training institutions like BCC, BITM, BIM etc. In last Phase, Practical Job Assignment will be on the job training in different companies for 3 months. more.....
